Cloudflare details how it built Cloudflare Queues on its own Developer Platform, evolving to a geographically-distributed, horizontally-scalable architecture built on Durable Objects. Median latency dropped from ~200ms to ~60ms and throughput increased from 400 to 5000 messages per second.
